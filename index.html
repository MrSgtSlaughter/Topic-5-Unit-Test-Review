Welcome, Parents! This guide covers everything on your child's Ancient China test. No prior knowledge needed — just follow the prompts on each card. Progress saves automatically to this device.
Step by step
1
Pick a mode at the top — Parent Guide reads prompts to you, Flashcards flip to reveal, Fill in Blank lets them type.
2
Pick a section — start with Vocabulary, then Key Figures, then continue in order.
3
Work each card — ask the question, wait for an answer, then reveal it together.
4
Mark it — "Got It!" or "Need to Review." Check the Score tab when done.
